Mission

Center for Advanced Parallel Systems (CAPS), also known as Parallel Systems Research Lab (PSRL), is currently focused on research and development of techniques and tools for automated learning, automated bug detection & correction for functionality as well as performance, and heterogeneous computing. Another important focus of the CAPS-PSRL is enabling and accelerating state-of-the-art algorithms targeting applied machine learning, data analytics, as well as privacy and security in blockchain and IoT systems
